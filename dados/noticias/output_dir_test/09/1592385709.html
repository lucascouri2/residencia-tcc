Wizz Air announces launch of five new flights from Lviv in July

29.04.2020 17:40
Ukrinform
Hungary’s low-cost airline Wizz Air has announced it will open five new flights from Lviv starting from July, the press service of Lviv International Airport reports on Facebook.

"Wizz Air airline announced the opening of five routes from Lviv. Flights will start operating from July," the report reads.

In particular, flights to Hamburg (Germany) will be carried out on Mondays and Fridays from July 3; to Tallinn (Estonia) every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday from July 2; to Billund (Denmark) on Wednesdays and Sundays from July 1; to Lisbon (Portugal) on Wednesdays and Sundays from July 1; to Szczecin (Poland) on Fridays and Mondays from July 3.

Also, from July 1, the Hungarian low-cost airline will open its base at the Lviv airport, where it will deploy Airbus A320 aircraft.
